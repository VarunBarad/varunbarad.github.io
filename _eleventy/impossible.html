I found out about the idea of an impossible list first from Thomas Frank. An impossible list is basically a challenge to yourself. It is a list of all things that I at one point of time thought I couldn't do simply because they were impossible (atleast for me). The challenge is to keep working towards these impossible things and chip away at them one bite at a time.

  • Perform 100 push-ups in a single set
  • Do a workout 100 days in a row
  • Write 500 words 100 days in a row
  • Do a skydive
  • Do a bungee jump
  • Ride on a Zero-G Flight
  • Jump around on a trampoline (Enjoyed my first trampoline session on 2023-05-17)
