Impossible List

I found out about the idea of an impossible list first from Thomas Frank. An impossible list is basically a challenge to yourself. It is a list of all things that I at one point of time thought I couldn't do simply because they were impossible (atleast for me). The challenge is to keep working towards these impossible things at chip away at them one bite at a time.

  • Perform 100 push-ups in a single set
  • Summit a mountain
  • Summit a snow-capped mountain
  • Make a completely home-grown meal
