DPTK Contacts
DP Trade-King is a stock-broking and wealth management firm based at Anand. They required an application which helped them to provide up-to-date contact information of each of their offices and employees both to their employees and their customers. So I made a desktop application using Kotlin which would parse data provided by them in Excel files and upload it to Firebase, from where it would be pulled down by the Android application for their use.
